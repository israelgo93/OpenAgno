# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want participation in the OpenAgno community to be respectful, safe, and constructive for everyone.

## Expected behavior

- Use respectful and inclusive language.
- Focus feedback on the work, not the person.
- Accept constructive criticism in good faith.
- Help keep the project useful for the broader community.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, threats, or intimidation
- Trolling or personal attacks
- Publishing private information without permission
- Discriminatory language or conduct

## Reporting

Report violations to `openagno@datatensei.com`.

This policy is adapted from the Contributor Covenant 2.1.
